## Core Technical Skills



I have hands-on experience with the hardware, software, and platforms that power modern IoT and robotic systems.



### Hardware & Prototyping

* *Microcontrollers:* Arduino (Uno, Nano), ESP32, ESP8266

* *Single-Board Computers:* Raspberry Pi (4, Zero W, Pico)

* *Sensors:* Ultrasonic, Infrared (IR), Temperature/Humidity (DHT11/22), Light Dependent Resistors (LDRs), MPU6050 (Accelerometer/Gyro)

* *Actuators:* Servo Motors, DC Motors (with L298N drivers), Stepper Motors, Relays

* *Prototyping:* Breadboarding, soldering, and circuit design



### Software & Protocols

* *Programming Languages:* *Python, **C/C++* (for Arduino), *MicroPython*

* *IoT Platforms:* ThingsSpeak, Blynk (basic)

* *Communication:* MQTT, HTTP, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth

* *Tools:* Arduino IDE, VS Code with PlatformIO
